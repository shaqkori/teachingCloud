Host a Website on Amazon S3

A complete beginner guide to deploying a static website on Amazon S3.

Step 1 — Create an S3 Bucket

Go to the AWS Console → S3 → Create bucket. Name your bucket the same as your domain (example: mywebsite.com).

Step 2 — Enable Static Website Hosting

Under your bucket settings, enable “Static website hosting” and choose index.html as the root file.

Step 3 — Upload Your Files

Upload your HTML, CSS, JS, and image files.

Step 4 — Allow Public Access

Update the bucket policy to allow public read access.

Step 5 — Open Your Website

You’ll get a public URL. Open it in your browser — your site is live!
